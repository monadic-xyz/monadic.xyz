Monadic

Monadic is a group of technologists who in 2017, set out to re-imagine free and open source software collaboration. Monadic is currently supporting the development of Radicle, a secure peer-to-peer alternative for code collaboration & accessp2p, an open learning program for introducing more people to the p2p space.
