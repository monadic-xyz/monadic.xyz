Monadic

At Monadic, the culture is the people we hire. We have core tenets, but these revolve principally around how we treat each other and what standards we try to meet.

We choose to work with people who are empathetic, humble, passionate about their work and strive for excellence.

Software Engineer, Radicle

Monadic is looking for an experienced senior software engineer to work on radicle, our peer-to-peer stack for code collaboration. Knowledge of either rust or haskell is highly desired. Since this is mostly protocol work, a background in network or systems programming is required.

You will have to keep up to date with recent developments in the field; the ability to read research papers and produce working implementations is essential. You will have to be an excellent communicator.

Software Engineer, Oscoin

Monadic is looking for an experienced software engineer to work on oscoin, a crypto-currency and network for code collaboration and value exchange in free and open-source software communities.

Knowledge of rust is required, as well as experience working with blockchain technologies at the protocol level. You will be tasked to build and deploy fault-tolerant distributed systems based on cryptographic proofs, authenticated data-structures and distributed-ledger technologies.

You will have to keep up to date with recent developments in the field; the ability to read research papers and produce working implementations is essential. You will have to be an excellent communicator.

Location

Our headquarters are located in Berlin, Germany, but we are a remote-friendly team with members all around Europe.

Compensation

Base compensation at Monadic is equal for all employees, including founders, regardless of location. We believe in having a level playing field for everyone and in keeping the team small.
