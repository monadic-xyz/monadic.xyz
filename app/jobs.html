Monadic

Culture

Our culture is grounded in the people we hire. We have core tenets which we all shape and evolve frequently, that primarily revolve around what standards we aim to meet when working together. We value a diverse and inclusive culture and we organize around the concepts of self-management and transparent decision making.

Location

Our headquarters are located in Berlin, Germany, but we are a remote-friendly team with members across Europe and North America.

Compensation

Base compensation at Monadic is equal for all team members, including founders. We believe in having a level playing field for everyone and in keeping the team small.

Software Engineer on Code Collaboration

The task

The Radicle Code Collaboration project is building a protocol for decentralised, peer-to-peer code sharing and collaboration. We research and work with distributed version control systems, peer-to-peer network protocols, data replication models, and asymmetric cryptography. Our goal is to provide a foundation for collaboration within the Free and Open Source Software community, which is owned by the community itself. As part of the Radicle ecosystem, higher-level applications and protocols will be building on our work.

We are looking for someone who has

  • excellent oral and written communication skills
  • an enthusiasm for open source software and collaboration more generally
  • experience using or desire to learn Rust (contributions to projects a big plus!)
  • enjoyment from designing and iterating on APIs

And it would be nice if you had at least one of the following

  • experience with distributed systems and peer-to-peer technology
  • experience working with the internals of VCSs such as Git, Pijul, Darcs
  • knowledge of blockchain technology
  • knowledge of cryptography
  • experience with network and systems programming

Software Engineer for Radicle Registry

The task

You’ll be working on the team building the *Radicle Registry*, a distributed ledger (blockchain) that serves as the coordination and economic layer for the Radicle platform and is written in Rust using Substrate. Within the team, you will own parts of the Registry Project and drive their development and success. As part of this project you will:

  • implement the ledger semantics of the Radicle Registry
  • work with Substrate to build the blockchain infrastructure
  • operate nodes for development and test networks
  • build clients and tools for the Registry
  • work with other teams to identify needs and requirements

We are looking for someone who has

  • experience using or desire to learn Rust
  • experience in designing and building software from scratch
  • the ability to drive and organize projects, self-manage, and make key decisions that balance multiple requirements and stakeholders
  • excellent oral and written communication skills

And it would be nice if you had at least one of the following

  • experience with blockchain technology, such as
    • building smart contracts
    • deploying, operating, and monitoring blockchain node clusters
  • familiarity with Substrate
  • Experience leading development on long-term projects, such as open source maintainership

Product Designer

The task

Currently, we’re hiring a second product designer to work with Julien to design the application that will serve as the interface to the code collaboration protocol. Due to the complexity of the peer-to-peer and crypto space, the product team regularly faces uncertainty and moving targets. This means that we do a lot of our product thinking through frequent conversations and feedback, often readjusting our assumptions. We’re looking for an independent, driven designer who will engage in and question our product thinking, but focus on visual & interaction design.

You will

  • own the design system and visual direction of the product, while contributing to overall product strategy and decision-making
  • create flows, prototypes, and high-fidelity visuals for new features
  • design features that ship quickly, with high quality
  • liaise closely with community, product & engineering teams to achieve the best possible results
  • be able to have fun with our brand and create a bunch of swag

We are looking for someone who has

  • strong visual design language
  • effective written and verbal communication skills
  • ability to think through complex interaction design
  • ability to express ideas through prototyping and/or code
  • creative thinking skills and a real passion for design
