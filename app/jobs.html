Monadic

At Monadic, our mission is to create a world where anyone has the opportunity to make a living through releasing their work in the open. Interested in joining us? We'd like to speak to people who are empathetic, humble, passionate about their work, and strive for excellence.

Culture

Core to our organization is self-management: we don’t have managers and we are individually responsible for scoping and fulfilling the work we contribute. As such, you will play an active role in defining how we achieve our purpose and be given opportunities to shape your role accordingly. Our culture is grounded in the people we hire. We have core tenets which you will uphold and help shape, which revolve primarily around what standards we aim to meet when working together. We’re a team that values a diverse and inclusive culture and strives for equality and transparency.

Location

Our headquarters are located in Berlin, Germany, but we are a remote-friendly team with members across Europe and North America

Compensation

Base compensation at Monadic is equal for all team members, including founders. We believe in having a level playing field for everyone and in keeping the team small.

Software Engineer on Code Collaboration

The Task

The Radicle Code Collaboration project is building a protocol for decentralised, peer-to-peer code sharing and collaboration. We research and work with distributed version control systems, peer-to-peer network protocols, data replication models, and asymmetric cryptography. Our goal is to provide a foundation for collaboration within the Free and Open Source Software community, which is owned by the community itself. As part of the Radicle ecosystem, higher-level applications and protocols will be building on our work.

You should have

  • excellent oral and written communication skills
  • an enthusiasm for open source software and collaboration more generally
  • proficiency in Haskell and/or Rust (contributions to projects a big plus!)
  • enjoyment from designing and iterating on APIs

You should also have at least one of the following

  • experience with distributed systems and peer-to-peer technology
  • experience working with the internals of VCSs such as Git, Pijul, Darcs
  • knowledge of blockchain technology
  • knowledge of cryptography
  • experience with network and systems programming

Software Engineer on Application

The Task

The Application project brings together our work on p2p code collaboration and the incentivization layer into one high-level, end-user experience. On the Application team, you will design client libraries for protocols based on p2p and blockchain technologies and integrate them into production-ready binaries to be used by a cross platform end-user application.

You should have

  • excellent oral and written communication
  • an enthusiasm for open source software and collaboration more generally
  • proficiency in Rust (contributions to projects a big plus!)
  • enjoyment from designing and iterating on APIs

You should also have at least one of the following

  • Experience with peer-to-peer technology
  • Knowledge of blockchain technology
  • Experience with GraphQL
  • A fundamental understanding of cryptography

Product Designer

The Task

Currently, we’re hiring a second product designer to help Julien design the application that will serve as the interface to the code collaboration protocol. Due to the complexity of the peer-to-peer and crypto space, the product team regularly faces uncertainty and moving targets. This means that we do a lot of our product thinking through frequent conversations and feedback, often readjusting our assumptions. We’re looking for an independent, driven designer who will engage in and question our product thinking, but focus on visual & interaction design.

You will

  • Own the design system and visual direction of the product, while contributing to overall product strategy and decision-making
  • Create flows, prototypes, and high-fidelity visuals for new features
  • Design features that ship quickly, with high quality
  • Liaise closely with community, product & engineering teams to achieve the best possible results
  • be able to have fun with our brand and create a bunch of swag

You should have

  • Strong visual design language
  • Effective written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to think through complex interaction design
  • Ability to express ideas through prototyping and/or code
  • Creative thinking skills and a real passion for design
