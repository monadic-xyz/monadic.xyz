Monadic

Culture

Our culture is grounded in the people we hire. We have core tenets which we all shape and evolve frequently, that primarily revolve around what standards we aim to meet when working together. We value a diverse and inclusive culture and we organize around the concepts of self-management and transparent decision making.

Location

Our headquarters are located in Berlin, Germany, but we are a remote-friendly team with members across Europe and North America.

Compensation

Base compensation at Monadic is equal for all team members, including founders. We believe in having a level playing field for everyone and in keeping the team small.
